About Tom

Hi, I’m Tommy Scholl. I love creating things that solve problems and elevate quality of life, and that drive has allowed me to succeed from a number of diverse vantage points within the tech industry. When I began in data and research, I pioneered new data management processes for bespoke software and eventually led research teams on-site and around the world. The startup world continued to fuel my love for collaboration and creativity and I pivoted to Digital Marketing, once again managing teams and working directly with leadership and the development teams to crush milestones and help them with each product sprint. Working so closely with SaaS products and dev teams, I knew I could make a bigger creative impact on tech companies if I became a dev myself.

I used my entire background to forge a new brand of freelance technology consulting, which allowed me to work on a deep level with software solutions for my clients while completing formal education on full-scale development. When I’m a part of a team, I bring more than a proficiency in Javascript, Python, CSS, HTML and various dev frameworks. My background allows me to break off the horse-blinders that some dev teams can become accustomed to on projects, and introduce a perspective that considers the wider picture. I am looking for a technology company that values a true generalist. In a technological world of increasing complexity, I have the skills and experience to bring clarity and simplicity - something that is often lost when those working in the code trenches are unable to come up for air and revisit the original goal of a project.
